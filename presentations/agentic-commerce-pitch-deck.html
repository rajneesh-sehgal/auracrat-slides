REFERENCE — SECTION 6
Why this is hard to copy

Three ideas, not a feature list

01
Adaptive, not fixed
The audit model is generated per merchant, per category. No universal SEO checklist.
02
Evidence & provenance
Every claim traces to merchant, manufacturer, or confirmed data. Unsupported inferences become questions, not facts.
03
A learning loop
Merchant approvals and corrections improve the model over time — an asset that gets harder to replace, not easier.
REFERENCE — SECTION 7
The ask is 90 days, not a platform

Three gates. Kill it early if the evidence says no

Days 1–30
Prove the problem
Smallest adaptive audit engine. Interview 10 merchants, 5 agencies, 3 catalog types.
Gate 1 — 3 catalog types produce meaningfully different rubrics; 3 prospects agree to a deeper pilot.
Days 31–60
Prove willingness to pay
Begin charging. Target 10 paid audits, $5,000 cumulative revenue.
Gate 2 — A large free user base is not enough. We need real dollars.
Days 61–90
Prove repeatability
Only build integrations paying customers demand. Target 5–10 recurring customers, 2 agency pilots.
Day 90 — Invest, Adjust, Partner, or Stop.
