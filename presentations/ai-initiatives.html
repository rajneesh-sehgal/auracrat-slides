01  ·  AI Agents & Knowledge
BizMind
AI agents that know your business and automate the work that drains your team
Vision

A small business gets its own AI workforce — agents that know how they operate and automate real workflows, not just answer questions.

Problem
  • Critical knowledge is scattered and never documented
  • Repetitive workflows eat hours that should go to customers
  • When key people are out, everything waits
How it works
  • Captures the business's knowledge, processes, and decision rules
  • Builds AI agents from reusable Skills that automate real workflows
  • Agents handle intake, follow-ups, scheduling, and reporting — without a human in the loop
Business model
  • Implementation fee plus monthly subscription
  • Each new workflow added increases switching cost
  • Their knowledge and agents are embedded — they won't leave
Why me
  • I map their actual workflows and build agents around how they specifically operate
  • Not a template. Not a chatbot. A system built for their business.
  • Local presence means agents get refined over time, not abandoned after launch
Growth potential
  • Skill library grows with every new client workflow I automate
  • Agents become more capable as they learn the business's patterns
  • Multi-location businesses and industry verticals are the natural next market

Growth potential
